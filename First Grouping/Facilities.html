Facilities

Our comshop offers a vibrant space with a spacious layout, state-of-the-art equipment, comfortable seating areas, and high-speed internet access. The facility is designed to be both functional and aesthetically pleasing, providing a great customer experience.

First Floor
The first floor features an open and functional layout with 5 computers each in the corners of the room. The room has two wall fans which is on the left and right front side of the room offering a well ventilated area for the customers.

This modern computers setup is designed for both gaming and productivity. At the center of the setup is a 27-inch 2k ASUS Monitor, offering visual clarity and vibrant colors. The monitor is connected to a high-end desktop PC with a liquid-cooled Intel Core i7 processor, 32GB of DDR4 RAM, and an NVIDIA RTX 3080 graphics card, ensuring smooth performance for tasks ranging from graphic design to gaming. The setup also includes a comfortable ergonomic chair with lumbar support, ensuring long hours at the desk remain pleasant.

Second Floor
As for the second it is actually meant for the VIP customers. Just like in the first floor it also has a 10 computers but the setup is different. As you can see, in the front corner 7 computers are lined up and the other three are on the left and right side corner. The room is air conditioned unlike in the first floor. Both floors follows a star like topology which is connected to a one server only.

This elite computer setup is engineered for those who demand uncompromising performance and aesthetics. At the core is a custom-built workstation featuring an Intel Core i9-13900K processor with 16 cores, paired with 64GB of Corsair Vengeance DDR5 RAM for lightning-fast multitasking and seamless handling of intensive applications, whether for 3D rendering, video editing, or extreme gaming. The powerhouse is further complemented by an NVIDIA RTX 4090 graphics card, delivering jaw-dropping visuals and capable of handling the most demanding rendering, gaming, and AI-based workloads without breaking a sweat.
